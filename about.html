About

Here's a bit of background on our company.

The Gloucester bag company is a family-run business established for nearly 10 years, building a reputation for supplying quality packaging solutions at competitive prices. We originally started as bespoke suppliers of quality products for the aquatic industry, This included the highest quality plastic bags and Cardboard boxes for transporting Koi carp around the UK.

We have since added many product lines to cater for other industries to include Butcher sundries, Bakers and Farm shops to mention a few. We pride ourselves on our close partnerships with manufacturers across the UK, Excellent customer service and the ability to supply our customers from all over the UK.

At The Gloucester Bag Company, we are dedicated to meeting the packaging requirements of your business whatever that may be, From full pallet deliveries to single item parcels, usually with overnight 24/48 hour service.

We can supply straight from our ever-growing stock items or from bespoke items tailored to your business requirements. Our friendly experienced Customer Support Team are here to manage your orders, provide quotations, product information and manage your orders from conception to delivery.
